Dr. Cameron Critchfield
Doctor of Dental Surgery

Dr. Critchfield is a native of McKinney, TX, and received a Bachelor’s degree from Brigham Young University. He graduated with honors in research from the University of Texas Health Science Center and completed a General Practice Residency through the University of Utah, where he received extensive training in implant placement and restoration, extractions, root canal therapy, and hospital dentistry. When not in the office, Dr. Critchfield loves spending time with his wife and kids, woodworking, running, playing soccer, and exploring beautiful Wyoming.

Jackie Brown
Office Manager

Jackie ensures the smooth operation of our practice, handling administrative tasks and coordinating patient care. Her organizational skills and friendly demeanor help create a positive experience for both patients and staff.

Lauren Williams
Registered Dental Hygienist

Lauren graduated from Sheridan College in 2007 and practiced dental hygiene in Colorado for 10 years before returning to her hometown of Cody, Wyoming. She has worked with Dr. Critchfield since 2020. Lauren loves patient interaction and believes that proper dental hygiene can be taught at any age.
