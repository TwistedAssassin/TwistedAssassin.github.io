We provide cleanings for children of all ages. Routine dental work is typically performed on children aged 10 and above. Younger children may be referred to a pediatric dentist if necessary.

All procedures that can be completed in our office at the standard of care or higher are included, such as fillings, crowns, root canals (excluding molars), implants, dentures, bone grafts, all types of cleanings, extractions, and Invisalign (esthetic and simple cases only).

If a procedure is required that we cannot perform in-house, you will be referred to a specialist. Any treatment not completed in our office is subject to the fees charged by the specialists.
