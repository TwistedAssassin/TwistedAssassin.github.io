Patient Privacy

Your trust matters to us. Learn how we protect your personal and health information.

  1. Home

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We follow all applicable federal and state laws, including HIPAA, to protect your personal and health information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Coordinate treatment and referrals
  • Submit insurance claims
  • Communicate with you about your care

How We Protect Your Information

We use administrative, physical, and technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your records
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Ask us to limit how we share your information, when permitted by law
  • Ask for a list of certain disclosures

Questions?

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please call (307) 587-0100 or email info@codysmiles.com.

Need Your Records?

To request a copy of your records, please call our office or submit a written request during your next visit.
