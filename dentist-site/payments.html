Payments

We offer a variety of convenient payment options to help make dental care accessible and affordable.

  1. Home

At Cody Family & Cosmetic Dentistry, we strive to make dental care accessible and affordable for all our patients. To accommodate your financial needs, we offer a variety of payment options:

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Cash
  • All Major Credit Cards
  • Most Insurance Plans
  • CareCredit
  • Cherry

Insurance Information

We accept all insurance plans except Medicare/Medicaid and are in-network with Cigna, Delta Dental, and Blue Cross Blue Shield. Our team is here to assist you with insurance verification and claims processing to help you maximize your benefits.

CodySmiles Initiative

To further assist our patients, we offer the CodySmiles Initiative, designed to make dental procedures more affordable. This program provides discounted rates on various dental services. Learn more on our CodySmiles Initiative page.

Payment Options

We offer several convenient ways to make payments:

  • In Person: Visit us at 1531 Bleistein Ave., Cody, WY 82414.
  • By Mail: Send payments to the address above.
  • By Phone: Call us at (307) 587-0100 to make a payment over the phone.
  • Online: Click here to pay online.

If you have any questions about our payment options or need assistance, please contact us at (307) 587-0100 or info@codysmiles.com.
