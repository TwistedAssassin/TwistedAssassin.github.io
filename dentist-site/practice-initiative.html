At Cody Family & Cosmetic Dentistry, we understand that dental care can be a significant financial commitment. To make essential dental services more accessible, we proudly offer the CodySmiles Initiative, a program designed to provide high-quality dental care at discounted rates.

Purpose of the CodySmiles Initiative

The CodySmiles Initiative aims to make dental procedures more affordable for everyone, regardless of income. By eliminating third-party involvement and offering direct, discounted pricing, we ensure that necessary dental treatments are within reach for all individuals.

Benefits of the CodySmiles Initiative

  • Affordable Pricing: Discounted rates often more cost-effective than traditional insurance.
  • No Maximum Benefit Limits: No annual maximums on benefits.
  • No Waiting Periods: Receive treatments promptly without delays.
  • Direct Access to Care: No third-party interference.
  • Universal Eligibility: Available to all individuals, regardless of income.

Program Commitment

By enrolling in the CodySmiles Initiative, you commit to:

  • Regular cleanings as recommended
  • Following your customized treatment plan
  • Maintaining the $30/month payment for 2 years

In return, we commit to:

  • Providing discounted services
  • Creating a plan for a healthy, beautiful smile
  • Performing all procedures at or above the standard of care
