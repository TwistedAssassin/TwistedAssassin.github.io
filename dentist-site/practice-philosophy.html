Practice Philosophy

Our practice philosophy centers on high-quality, affordable dental care tailored to your unique needs.

  1. Home
  2. About
  3. Practice Philosophy

At Cody Family & Cosmetic Dentistry, our practice philosophy centers on providing high-quality, affordable dental care tailored to each patient’s unique needs. We are committed to making dentistry accessible and stress-free, ensuring that every individual achieves optimal oral health and a confident smile.

Personalized Care

We believe in treating each patient as a unique individual, taking the time to understand your specific dental concerns and goals. Our team develops customized treatment plans that align with your needs, preferences, and budget, ensuring the best possible outcomes.

Affordability and Accessibility

To make dental care more accessible, we offer a New Patient Special for our cash-paying patients, which includes a free exam and X-rays with your first cleaning. We accept all insurances except Medicare/Medicaid and are in-network with Cigna, Delta Dental, and Blue Cross Blue Shield. Our CodySmiles Initiative further reflects our dedication to making dentistry affordable for everyone.

Comprehensive Services

Our practice offers a wide range of services, from preventive care to advanced cosmetic and restorative treatments. By providing comprehensive dental care under one roof, we aim to meet all your oral health needs in a convenient and comfortable setting.

Community Commitment

Located in Cody, Wyoming, we are proud to serve our local community. We strive to build lasting relationships with our patients, fostering a sense of trust and partnership in achieving long-term oral health.

We are dedicated to enhancing your quality of life through exceptional dental care, personalized attention, and a commitment to your overall well-being.

Our Philosophy at a Glance
  • Patient-first, personalized care
  • Affordable options & transparent pricing
  • Comprehensive services under one roof
  • Commitment to the Cody community

We want every visit to feel comfortable, clear, and focused on your long-term health.
